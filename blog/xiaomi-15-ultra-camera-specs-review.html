Xiaomi 15 Ultra Review: The 200MP Periscope Camera King

📅 July 9, 2026 • 📝 PixabAnimation Team
**Introduction: A Photography Powerhouse Without Compromise** The Xiaomi 15 Ultra is designed for photography enthusiasts who demand the absolute best image quality from a smartphone. Its centerpiece is a staggering 200MP periscope telephoto camera paired with a 1-inch type 50MP main sensor — the largest sensor in any mainstream smartphone.
