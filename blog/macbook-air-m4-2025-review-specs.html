MacBook Air M4 (2025): Apple Intelligence for Everyone — Complete Review

📅 July 9, 2026 • 📝 PixabAnimation Team
**Introduction: The Ultimate Ultraportable Gets Smarter** The MacBook Air M4 represents Apple's most popular Mac with the M4 chip, bringing significant performance improvements, Apple Intelligence capabilities, and most importantly, making 16GB of unified memory standard across all configurations.
