Best Motion Graphics Stock Video Resources in 2026

📅 March 7, 2026•👁️ 29.4K+ Views•📝 PixabAnimation Team

High-quality stock video resources are essential for motion designers who need to work efficiently without compromising quality. Whether you need motion backgrounds, animated templates, or 4K footage, the right stock resource platform can dramatically accelerate your workflow. This guide covers the best motion graphics stock video resources available in 2026.

Premium Stock Video Platforms

Several platforms offer high-quality motion graphics stock assets. Storyblocks provides unlimited downloads with a subscription model, making it ideal for high-volume creators. Artgrid offers curated, cinematic footage with a focus on authentic storytelling. Pond5 features a vast marketplace with both premium and budget options. Adobe Stock integrates directly with After Effects and Premiere Pro for seamless workflow.

Motion Graphics Templates

Pre-built templates save hours of production time. Envato Elements offers unlimited downloads of After Effects templates, video assets, and design resources. VideoHive provides individual template purchases. Motion Array combines template downloads with project management tools. Looka and Placeit offer template-based logo animation and branding tools.

Free Stock Resources

Quality free resources are available for designers on a budget. Mixkit offers free motion graphics templates and stock videos. Coverr provides free background videos. Videvo offers a mix of free and premium clips. LottieFiles offers free animated icons and illustrations in Lottie format. These resources are excellent for practice projects, personal work, and budget-constrained commercial projects.

Choosing the Right Platform

Selecting the right platform depends on your specific needs. For high-volume production, subscription services like Envato Elements or Storyblocks offer the best value. For occasional projects, pay-per-asset platforms like Pond5 or VideoHive are more economical. Consider integration with your existing tools — Adobe Stock integrates seamlessly with Creative Cloud. Evaluate licensing terms carefully for commercial projects.
